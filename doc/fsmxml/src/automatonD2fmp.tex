    The transducer~$\mathcal{D}_{2}$ that realises the Fibonacci reduction 
    $a\, b\, b \rightarrow b\, a\, a$, viewed as an automaton on 
    $\left\{ a,b \right\}* \times \left\{ a,b \right\}*$.
